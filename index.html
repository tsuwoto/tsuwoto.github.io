Find MYcrowave

About

Pretty much every student at Georgia Tech knows there is a microwave or two in the student center, but if you bring your lunch and are looking to microwave it, you don't want to stand in that line for the microwave! There's got to be more microwaves on campus, right?

Look no more! With Find MYcrowave, you can easily find all microwave locations on campus. Just click on the marker and it will tell you the exact details of where to find that microwave. Bon appetit!
